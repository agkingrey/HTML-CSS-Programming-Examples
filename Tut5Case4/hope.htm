Welcome to A New Hope

A Modest Introduction

Hello There curious observer, and welcome to a New Hope. This planet is humanitie's next evolution. Here we will start again with green technology, smart governance, and equality for all. This planet is a way for humanity to start fresh, free of the prejudice, hatred, and greed of earth. Here, every citizen will have a voice, and everyone will be happy.

Your part in this new adventure

Every citizen in a New Hope will have a voice in the governing of the planet. Every single decision will come down to a majority vote, and the will of the people will be heard. There are a few ground rules that need to be set beforehand though. 1. Every citizen must work until they are 60. 2. Every citizen must vote in every decision. 3. Every citizen must wear a purple hat at all time.

How A New Hope was discovered

New Hope was discovered by the great astronaut, William PurpleHat. As Mr. PurpleHat was cruizing the stars, he saw a blue blip in the distance. All of his sensors pointed towards life on the planet, so he went to investigate. He found beautiful plains, odd insects, and many animals, both friendly and hostile.
